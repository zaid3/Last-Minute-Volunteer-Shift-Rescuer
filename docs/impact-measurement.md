# Impact measurement plan

The repository does not claim operational impact before a real charity pilot produces evidence.

## Baseline

Before the pilot, record how the charity currently fills urgent shifts:

- average number of calls or messages per urgent vacancy
- median time from cancellation to confirmed replacement
- percentage of urgent vacancies filled
- coordinator time spent per incident
- number of duplicate or unclear confirmations

## Pilot measures

For each broadcast, record:

- time the vacancy was created
- time alerts were sent
- number of volunteers alerted
- time of the first successful confirmation
- whether the shift was filled
- delivery failures
- coordinator intervention required

## Outcome measures

Compare the pilot period with the baseline using:

- median time to confirmed cover
- urgent-shift fill rate
- coordinator minutes saved per incident
- volunteer response rate
- email delivery success rate
- coordinator and volunteer satisfaction

## Evidence quality

Any published result should identify the measurement period, number of shifts, calculation method and source. Testimonials should be dated and attributable to the person providing them. Personal volunteer data must be removed from public reports.
