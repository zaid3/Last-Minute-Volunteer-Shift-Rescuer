# Problem statement

Charities depend on volunteers to deliver scheduled services. When a volunteer cancels shortly before a shift or does not arrive, a coordinator must find replacement cover quickly.

Common manual approaches include calling volunteers one by one, sending messages to group chats or maintaining informal backup lists. These approaches create several problems:

- contacting people sequentially increases response time
- several volunteers may believe they have accepted the same shift
- coordinators lack a consistent record of who was contacted and who responded
- messages may not clearly show whether the shift remains available
- personal contact details may be copied across multiple uncontrolled channels

The product objective is to reduce the coordination steps between identifying an urgent gap and assigning one confirmed volunteer, while maintaining a clear record of the decision.

The product does not replace safeguarding, suitability checks, rota policy or human oversight. It only supports the urgent communication and assignment workflow.
